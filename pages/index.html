Install

Finch installs to your device so you can open anytime. Pick your device below.

Why install this way? Because Finch is meant to stay lightweight. We do not want to insert ourselves into your system, track you through other apps, or hog your device resources. It is a simple tool you open when you need it, then leave alone when you do not.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open this page in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow) then more.
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open Finch from your Home Screen.
If you don’t see “Add to Home Screen,” you’re probably not in Safari.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open this page in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu.
  3. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen.
  4. Open Finch from your home screen.

Windows / Mac (Chrome or Edge / Safari)

  1. Open this page in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Click the Install icon in the address bar (or menu).
  3. Confirm install.
  4. Mac Safari Tap the Share button (square with arrow) and add to Dock.
Tip: on Mac, you can drag it to the Dock from Applications.
